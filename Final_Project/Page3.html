How I started

All my life always envied shoes, but I never had the money to be buying them. When I was 14, I finally saved enough to buy a pair. I went to a store to buy them, and they were on sale, $100 instead of $180. They had my size, and I ended up buying a pair. A few days later I hadn't of worn them because I didn't like them as much as I did, so I sold them on ebay for $150.
