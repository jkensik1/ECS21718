Rosa Parks stood up as a minority.

African Americans had been segregated and mistreated at the time so they couldn't sit on the bus if a white person didn't already have a seat to sit in.
Rosa Parks remained seated when a white person told them to stand up, and she was arrested but it raised awareness.
