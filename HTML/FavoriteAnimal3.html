Ligres, as described in the name, look exactly like a mix of a tiger and a lion


Ligres are also very large in size.

Ligre's patterns can also vary.

History
The history of ligre hybrids date to the early 19th century in India. In 1798, Étienne Geoffroy Saint-Hilaire made a color plate of the offspring of a lion and a tiger.
